Everything you need to know before working with Cho&Co.

Data cleaning is the process of detecting and correcting corrupt, inaccurate, duplicate, or improperly formatted records in a dataset. Businesses need clean data because flawed data leads to flawed decisions, missed trends, incorrect forecasts, and wasted spend. Cho&Co removes duplicates, standardises formats, and validates accuracy so your data is ready for reliable, confident analysis.
Most analytics dashboards are delivered within 5–10 business days depending on the complexity and volume of your data. We provide real-time, interactive dashboards with custom KPI tracking tailored specifically to your business goals and reporting requirements.
Cho&Co offers affordable, transparent pricing customised per project, starting from R5000. We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to established enterprises. Contact us or book a free demo to receive a no-obligation quote based on your specific data needs and scope.
We work with CSV, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), and most standard business data formats. During the initial assessment phase, we analyse your current data structure and adapt our process to whatever format your business already uses, no technical expertise required from your side.
No technical knowledge is required. Cho&Co handles all the technical complexity, including data wrangling, analysis, and visualisation. We deliver clear, easy-to-understand dashboards and reports that your whole team can use, regardless of technical background.
